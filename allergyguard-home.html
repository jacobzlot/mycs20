About Allergy Guard:

At Allergy Guard, our mission is to allow you to explore the amazing restaurants your city has to offer without the stress involved with doing so.

How to get started:
  • Navigate to Restaurants
  • Select your allergens
  • Select your city
  • Allergy Guard will provide you with a list of allergen-free or allergen-friendly restaurants
  • Reserve your booking and have a lovely, safe meal with your family!
